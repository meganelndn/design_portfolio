03.02.01/05 Copenhagen Bike Guide

FOCUS Creating a micro website for hosting a Copenhagen bike guide video. The goal was to create a short video for a particular target audience, introducing them to the bike life in Copenhagen.

Creating a storyboard for the video to plan the camera angles, shot lenght and other aspects. Filming the video using cameras and audio equipement.

Creating a high fidelity prototype and then a website following the rules of the Copenhagen City style guide.


PERSONAL ROLE Coding the website. Learning additional elements of code - adding video files to HTML and formatting them. Creating a fully responsive website using grids and media queries.

GAINS Learning about the different dramaturgical elements relevant to creation of a short video/film - the difference between shots, the importance of composition, colours, and audio effects.

Work with cameras, audio equipment and sound and video editing programs - Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe Audition.

Learning to film on location, plan shots, distribute tasks and overcome real life challenges and difficulties in the filming process.
